Upload an inspection report from Ofsted. The app will predict whether or not the school is in danger of closing by determining the possibility that the report will be the last published report before the school closes. The probability that appears represents how confident the classifier is in its decision. You can also view the words that help the classifier make its prediction highlighted in purple below. The darker the highlight, the more significant the word is to our machine learning model. This app is powered by fast.ai and was built by the Quartz AI Studio in collaboration with the BBC.
